Question 1 of 5
Right now, how many options or paths are you actively pursuing at the same time?
A lot of people arrive in the Netherlands with a clear plan, then find things look different in practice. It's easy to end up exploring several directions at once without committing to any of them.
By the end, you'll know exactly what is slowing you down, and one thing to do about it.
These questions work for any field and any background. There are no right or wrong answers, just pick the one that feels most true.
Question 2 of 5
Think about the last few useful connections or conversations you've had here, whether at work, socially, or professionally. How did most of them happen?
In the Netherlands, both professional and social networks tend to work the same way: things move through people, not platforms. This is true whether you're looking for work, trying to build a social life, or both.
These questions work for any field and any background. There are no right or wrong answers, just pick the one that feels most true.
Question 3 of 5
When someone asks what you do or what you're looking for here. What usually happens?
What made you stand out in your previous country doesn't always come across the same way here. It's not about your skills. It's about whether the people you talk to can quickly understand what you bring.
These questions work for any field and any background. There are no right or wrong answers, just pick the one that feels most true.
Question 4 of 5
Looking at last week. Which of these best describes how you spent your energy?
Being busy and moving forward are not the same thing. If your effort is scattered across too many things, nothing really builds. This question looks at whether your energy is focused enough to create real momentum.
These questions work for any field and any background. There are no right or wrong answers, just pick the one that feels most true.
Question 5 of 5
When you think about what's actually making this transition hard right now. What fits best?
Long transitions are tiring in more ways than one. It's not just about work: feeling disconnected or isolated socially can drain your energy just as much as professional uncertainty.
These questions work for any field and any background. There are no right or wrong answers, just pick the one that feels most true.

Privacy & Data
How we handle your data
What we store
Your first name, email address, and your scan result (which layer scored highest, plus all 5 layer scores). That's it. We don't track your behavior, location, or anything else.
Why we store it
So we can send you a copy of your result, and follow up only if it's useful. If you opted in to the newsletter, we may send occasional insights for internationals in NL.
Where it's stored
Securely via Formspree, a GDPR-compliant data processor based in the EU. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
How long we keep it
As long as it's relevant to our communication with you. You can request deletion at any time and we'll remove it within 7 days.
Your rights
Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email hi@fluxforward.world and we'll respond within 7 days.
